AUTONOMOUS NAVIGATION ARCHITECTURAL UPGRADE SPECIFICATION

PHASE 1: PHOTOMETRIC AUGMENTATION & STOCHASTIC DOMAIN RANDOMIZATION
- Target: train/data_loaders.py
- Implementation: Torchvision ColorJitter and Gaussian Stochastic Noise Injection.
- Rationale: Mitigation of the Sim-to-Real gap by decorrelating control policy from environment-specific RGB manifolds. Forces ResNet-18 backbone to converge on geometric invariants.

PHASE 2: RECURRENT TEMPORAL INTEGRATION (NON-MARKOVIAN STATE PERSISTENCE)
- Target: drone_nav/perception/encoders.py & train/trainer.py
- Implementation: Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU) with 256-dimensional Hidden State (h_t).
- Rationale: Resolution of Temporal Occlusion during high-yaw maneuvers. Facilitates information persistence across the 10-frame sliding window, preventing control instability when landmarks are momentarily obscured.

PHASE 3: DIFFERENTIABLE GOAL TERMINATION & LOCAL POTENTIAL FIELDS (VFH)
- Target: drone_nav/control/planner.py
- Implementation: 
    1. Binary Classifier Head (Logits) for Learned Mission Termination.
    2. Vector Field Histogram (VFH) derived from Monocular Depth Inversion.
- Rationale:
    - Replaces heuristic similarity thresholds with a learned probability distribution for Landing.
    - Transitions from Binary Collision Triggers to Repulsive Gradient Vectors, optimizing Local Trajectory Perturbation and Average Completion Time (ACT).

CRITICAL PERFORMANCE METRICS:
- Mission Success Probability (SR): Target > 0.50
- Efficiency-Success Score (ESS): Target > 0.40
- Path Deviation (MSE): Target < 0.1 m/s
