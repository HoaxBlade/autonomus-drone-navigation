DRONE NAVIGATION UPGRADE PLAN (FACTUAL)

PHASE 1: ROBUST PERCEPTION (SIM-TO-REAL)
- Target: data_loaders.py
- Task: Add Photometric Augmentation.
- Details: ColorJitter (brightness=0.2, contrast=0.2, saturation=0.1) and GaussianNoise.
- Purpose: Force the model to learn geometric shapes instead of simulation colors.

PHASE 2: TEMPORAL REASONING (MEMORY)
- Target: encoders.py & trainer.py
- Task: Implement Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU).
- Details: Add a 256-dim hidden state to the perception backbone.
- Purpose: Prevent the "blind spot" issue where the drone forgets obstacles once they leave the immediate frame.

PHASE 3: DECISION INTELLIGENCE (STRATEGIST)
- Target: planner.py
- Task: Learned Landing & Vector Field Histograms (VFH).
- Details: 
    1. Replace hardcoded similarity thresholds with a learned landing classifier.
    2. Replace "Emergency Stop" with "Repulsive Vectors" derived from the depth map.
- Purpose: Increase Success Rate (SR) and allow for swerving around obstacles.

METRICS FOR EVALUATION:
- Success Rate (SR): Goal > 50%
- Efficiency-Success Score (ESS): Goal > 0.4
- Deviation Rate: Target < 0.1 m/s
