PHASE 7: HIGH-FIDELITY CLOSED-LOOP SIMULATION

OVERVIEW:
Transition from offline replaying to a high-fidelity, closed-loop simulation designed to minimize the sim-to-real gap.

1. SIMULATOR BRIDGE (Habitat-Sim)
- Photorealistic 3D environment loading (Gibson/HM3D).
- Timing: Fixed 30Hz control loop with 10Hz perception updates.
- Data: Real-time RGB and Depth frame rendering.

2. SECOND-ORDER DYNAMICS
- State Tracking: position [x,y,z] and velocity [vx,vy,vz].
- Physics Logic: v_t = v_t-1 + (acceleration - damping * velocity) * dt.
- Realistic constraints for max speed and motor response latency.
- Safety: Identical emergency braking logic to the physical drone (1.5m margin).

3. EPISODE STRUCTURE
- Reset: Randomized start and goal coordinate pairs for every trial.
- Failure: Episode ends on collision or exceeding 500 steps (Timeout).
- Success: Reaching within 1.0m of the goal coordinate.

4. LOGGING AND VISUALIZATION
- Step Logging: Save position, velocity, and goal distance to HDF5/CSV.
- Visualization: Top-down trajectory overlay and real-time goal heatmap.

5. METRICS
- Success Rate (SR): Percentage of completed missions.
- SPL: Success weighted by Path Length (Efficiency metric).
