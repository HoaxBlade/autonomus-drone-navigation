PHASE 7: CLOSED-LOOP SIMULATION ROADMAP (v2.5)

OVERVIEW:
Transition from offline dataset replaying (TartanAir) to real-time, closed-loop simulation where the drone's actions actively change its environment.

OBJECTIVES:
1. Implement a real-time simulator bridge (Habitat-Sim).
2. Create a drone dynamics controller for realistic movement.
3. Establish a continuous navigation loop for autonomous flight.

COMPONENTS:
- habitat_bridge.py: Handles 3D rendering (RGB/Depth) and environment interaction.
- dynamics.py: Calculates pose updates based on velocity, inertia, and physics.
- sim_runner.py: The main engine that ties perception, planning, and simulation together.

VERIFICATION:
- Performance: Measure success rate of autonomously reaching 3D goal coordinates.
- Safety: Verify the obstacle avoidance system triggers emergency braking in 3D space.
- Efficiency: Calculate the success weighted by path length (SPL).

STATUS:
Planning Complete. Ready for Implementation.
